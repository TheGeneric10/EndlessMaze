❓ HOW TO PLAY

MOVEMENT

W A S D
Move through the maze. Each key press moves one cell.
↑ ↓ ← →
Arrow keys also move the player.
ESC / P
Pause the game and access run options.

OBJECTIVES

🪙 COINS
Walk over coins to collect them. Spend in the shop between runs.
💎 DIAMONDS
Rare currency found in vaults. Used for premium upgrades.
🎁 CHESTS
Walk over chests to open them. They contain random rewards.
FLOOR
Go deeper to reach higher floors. Each floor is harder and more rewarding.

SPECIAL ROOMS

🚩 CHECKPOINT
Glowing green rooms. Walk in to save your position in the maze.
💜 MERCHANT
Purple rooms. Buy items mid-run using your collected coins.
🏆 TREASURE
Gold-lit rooms with extra chests and diamonds.
💙 SAFE ROOM
Blue rooms. No traps. Can restore 1 HP.

TRAPS & HAZARDS

⚠ SPIKE
Static red floor. Takes 1 damage on entry. Watch the floor color.
🔄 SAW
Moving blade that patrols the room. Avoid its path.
🌫 MIST
Poison mist. Take damage each second you remain in the room.

CHECKPOINTS & SAVES

The game auto-saves when you reach checkpoints. You can manually save from the pause menu. When you return to the main menu, you can load from your last checkpoint, safe room, or start a fresh run. Progress like coins, upgrades, and quests is always saved between sessions.

QUESTS & PINNING

Quests give you specific goals. Pin up to 3 quests — they'll show on your HUD during runs so you can track progress live. Complete quests to earn bonus coins, diamonds, cosmetics, and permanent upgrades.

DIFFICULTY

RELAXED
Fewer traps. More coins. Good for learning the maze.
STANDARD
Balanced experience. Default mode.
BRUTAL
Dense traps, fewer safe rooms, harder enemies. Max rewards.
